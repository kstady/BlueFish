Adding Integers with the Same Sign

When adding numbers with the same sign, there is no “battle.” Instead, the numbers join forces to create a larger asset or a deeper debt.

Joining Assets

If you have \(\$400\) in your savings account (\(+400\)) and you win \(\$150\) (\(+150\)), you have a net balance of $550.

\[ 400 + 150 = 550 \]
Joining Debts

If you owe your brother \(\$10\) (\(-10\)) and then borrow another \(\$25\) (\(-25\)), your net balance is a debt of \(\$35.\) So, you are falling deeper into debt.

\[ -10 + (-25) = -35 \]

We can now write a formal mathematical rule for adding integers with the same sign. Once again, we will use absolute value.

Steps for Adding Integers with the Same Signs
  1. Find the absolute value or strength of each number.
  2. Add the absolute values.
  3. Write the answer with the common sign.

Use the three-step process to find the sum \(-50 + (-30).\)

Step 1

Find the Absolute Values (Strengths):
Fnd the strength of each number.

\[ |-50| = 50 \quad \text {and} \quad |30| = 30 \]
Step 2

Add the Absolute Values (Join Forces):
Since the signs are the same, we add their absolute values.

\[ 50 + 30 = 80 \]
Step 3

Determine the Sign and Write the Answer:
Since both numbers are negative, the answer is negative.

\[ -50 + (-30) = -80 \]
Apply the steps to find the sum \(-15 + (-40).\)

 

Step 1

Find the Absolute Values (Strengths):

\[ |-15| = \answer {15} \quad \text {and} \quad |-40| = \answer {40} \]
Step 2

Add the Absolute Values (Join Forces):

\[ 15 + 40 = \answer {55} \]
Step 3

Determine the Sign and Write the Answer:

 \(-15 + (-40) = \answer {-55}\)

A research submarine is exploring the ocean floor. It starts at a depth of \(5\) meters below sea level. The pilot then navigates the submarine down another \(8\) meters. Find the submarine’s final depth relative to sea level.
\[ -5 + (-13) = \answer {-18} \ \textrm {meters} \]
During a football game, the home team’s running back gained \(6\) yards on the first play of the drive. On the very next play, he charged forward an additional \(7\) yards. Find the total number of yards gained by the running back over these two plays.
\[ 6 + 7 = \answer {13} \ \textrm {yards} \]
On a cold winter night in Cleveland, the starting temperature at midnight was \(-1^\circ \text {F}\). Over the next three hours, another cold front moved through the area and the temperature dropped by \(9^\circ \text {F}.\) Find the temperature at 3am.
\[ -1 + (-9) = \answer {-10} \ ^\circ \text {F} \]
